About Us

Our project aims to create a food ordering system for our school. This will allow students to preorder food that they plan to eat before they reach the canteen. As such, this allows store owners to better plan and prepare for the number of orders that they may be receiving. Additionally, students will not need to queue up in front of stalls in order to order their meals, thus reducing this inconvenience.
